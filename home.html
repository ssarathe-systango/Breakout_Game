Break Out Game


Instruction

  • Need to register firstly yourself.
  • To move paddle use left and right arrow keys.
  • As you cleared the levels difficulty is also increased.
  • Different color of the bricks have different powers.
  • Bricks of White color are normal.
  • Bricks of Green color will increase life.
  • Bricks of Red color will increase ball speed.
  • Bricks of Blue color will increase size of paddle.
  • Bricks of Yellowcolor will decrease size of paddle.
